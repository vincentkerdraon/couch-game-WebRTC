This is a Proof of Concept demonstrating the use of WebRTC technology for real-time communication in a multiplayer couch gaming experience.

What is the Point?

The goal of this POC is to showcase how WebRTC can be used to enable seamless peer-to-peer communication, allowing players to connect and interact in real-time without the need for a centralized server.

How to Test

  1. Ensure you have a modern web browser that supports WebRTC (e.g., Chrome, Firefox).
  2. Open this application in your browser, for example on your smart-TV.
  3. Navigate to the "host" part.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to connect with another player, for example with your phone.
  5. Test the real-time communication features.

Thank you for exploring this POC. Your feedback is appreciated!
